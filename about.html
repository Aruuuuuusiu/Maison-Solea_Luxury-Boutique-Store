A legacy of craftsmanship

Three decades of dedication to the art of shoemaking

Our Beginning

Founded in 1990 by master craftsman Alessandro Moretti, Maíson Solea was born from a singular vision: to create shoes that transcend mere function and become treasured companions in life's most important moments.

What began in a small Florentine workshop has grown into a distinguished house known for its unwavering commitment to quality and timeless design.

Our Values

The principles that guide every creation

Craftsmanship

Each pair is handcrafted by artisans who have honed their skills over decades. We employ traditional techniques passed down through generations, ensuring every stitch reflects our dedication to excellence.

Quality Materials

We source only the finest leathers and materials from sustainable suppliers around the world. Every component is carefully selected to ensure durability, comfort, and timeless beauty.

Timeless Design

Our designs eschew fleeting trends in favor of enduring elegance. We create shoes that remain relevant and beautiful for years, becoming cherished pieces in your wardrobe.

Sustainability

We believe luxury and responsibility go hand in hand. Our commitment to sustainable practices ensures that beauty today doesn't compromise tomorrow.
